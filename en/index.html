About

About Neureps

Neureps Inc. is a software startup founded in 2026 in Yongin, South Korea. We design and build mobile apps for everyday life, and weave machine learning and generative AI naturally into our products.

We think technology is a lot like a tree. Plant it in the right place, tend it steadily, and one day it gives back shade and fruit. The name Neureps and our open-armed tree logo carry that promise: to build software that keeps growing for a long time.

We are currently working on our first app. We are a small team, but we intend to see everything we make through to the end.
