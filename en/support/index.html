How to reach us

Whether it's something that bothered you while using an app, a bug report, or a feature idea — every message is welcome. Write to the address below and we will reply within 2 business days.

  • Email: contact@neureps.com

Frequently asked questions

Q. What are the ads shown in the apps? Free apps by Neureps are supported by ads served through Google AdMob. For how personal information is handled in connection with ads, please see our privacy policy.

Q. I want my data deleted. Email us a deletion request and we will verify it and act without delay. Including the name of the app you use helps us handle it faster.

Q. I found a bug. Email us your device model, operating system version, and what you were doing when the problem occurred, and we will make fixing it a top priority for the next update.
